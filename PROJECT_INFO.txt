TROUBLESHOOTING
â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•

Problem: "SUMO_HOME not set"
Solution: export SUMO_HOME=/usr/share/sumo
          (adjust path to your SUMO installation)

Problem: "netconvert not found"
Solution: sudo apt-get install sumo-tools
          or ensure $SUMO_HOME/tools is in PATH

Problem: "Module 'traci' not found"
Solution: export PYTHONPATH="$SUMO_HOME/tools:$PYTHONPATH"

Problem: "Permission denied: ./run.sh"
Solution: chmod +x run.sh

For more help, see: docs/INSTALLATION.md
